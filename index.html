How I Think

Product maturity
over flashy demos.

01
Invisible AI

AI should remove friction, not announce itself. The best AI feature is one users don't notice — they just feel like the app understood them.

02
Constraint Thinking

One thing done deeply beats ten things done poorly. I scope products ruthlessly — then execute that scope with obsessive polish.

03
Onboarding First

Most builders skip the first-use experience. I obsess over it. Getting someone to their first success fast is the entire game.

04
Design for Failure

Weak builders present AI as magic. I design around latency, uncertainty, and graceful degradation — because real users encounter all three.

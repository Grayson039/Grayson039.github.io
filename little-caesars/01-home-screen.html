✅ Redesign Fixes
Store always visible
Location pill in the header. One tap to change. Users never lose their context.
Hot-N-Ready front & center
Live status + wait time is the first card. Their brand promise made actionable.
Full menu above the fold
Category grid lets users browse before committing to an order. Zero friction to explore.
Promo right-sized
One card, not a full-screen takeover. Campaign gets visibility â€” utility wins.
Reorder is real
Two recent items visible, one tap to re-add. Repeat orders drive retention and revenue.
One nav, no hamburger
Raised ORDER button = clear primary CTA. No drawer, no confusion, no second-guessing.
Design Decision
Orange stays dominant
Full orange header preserves the brand's visual identity. White content cards below create a clear "brand zone vs content zone" â€” the transition feels intentional, not accidental.
Business Impact
Hot-N-Ready drives conversion
Showing real-time availability eliminates the #1 drop-off: users not knowing if pizza is ready and leaving. Live status creates urgency and the confidence to complete an order.

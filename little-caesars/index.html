Design Process
How We Got Here
01
Audit the Real App
Started with 9 actual iOS screenshots of the live Little Caesars app. Documented every friction point, dead-end, and missed opportunity before touching any design tool.
02
Identify the Core Failures
The home screen is 100% promotional. The menu is 4 taps away. There are two competing nav systems. Hot-N-Ready — their biggest brand differentiator — is invisible on launch.
03
Redesign for Utility First
Every decision starts with: what does the user actually need right now? Status, menu access, and reorders above the fold. Promotions right-sized to one card — not a full-screen takeover.
